BDD Concepts

BDD revolves around the concept of a Scenario, which at its core comprises of a number of executed Steps. The Steps can be of three types:

  • Given represents a precondition to an event
  • When represents an event
  • Then represents an outcome of the event

A Story or a Feature, in turn, comprises of one of more Scenarios.
